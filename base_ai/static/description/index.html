Functionality:

"Purchase Digitalization" streamlines manual work by automatically adding identifiable descriptions for text fields in Odoo. It uses OCR to extract information, updates various fields, and configures different AI models. The module enhances productivity and efficiency by generating automatic responses within the Odoo social network and other applications.



Benefits:

  • Saves Time: Automates the process of digitizing purchase, significantly reducing manual effort and saving valuable time.
  • Cost-Efficient: Reduces the costs associated with manual data entry and minimizes the risk of errors, leading to overall cost savings.
  • Improved Accuracy: Utilizes OCR technology and AI to enhance accuracy in extracting and populating invoice details.
  • Streamlined Processes: Streamlines the workflow by automatically updating Odoo records with extracted information.
